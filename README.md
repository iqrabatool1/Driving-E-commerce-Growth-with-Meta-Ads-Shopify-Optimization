# Driving E-commerce Growth with Meta Ads & Shopify Optimization

This project showcases a complete e-commerce growth strategy combining **Meta Ads campaigns (Facebook & Instagram)**, **Google Ads**, and **Shopify store optimization** to increase sales, improve UX, and drive measurable results.

---

## My Role

- Managed Meta Ads campaigns on Facebook & Instagram.  
- Implemented Shopify store optimizations and automation.  
- Analyzed performance data and optimized campaigns based on results.  
- Ran Google Ads campaigns and boosted Instagram Reels.  

---

## Objectives

- Increase overall sales of the client’s online store.  
- Effectively manage Meta Ads campaigns using **data-driven strategies**.  
- Track conversions accurately through **Meta Pixel setup**.  
- Improve Shopify store functionality with smart plugins, automation, and optimized catalog.  
- Boost organic engagement and reach through Instagram Reels and social media campaigns.  
- Streamline e-commerce workflow combining marketing, analytics, and store management.

## Meta Pixel Setup

**Purpose:**  
- Track conversions accurately  
- Measure ad performance  
- Optimize campaigns based on visitor actions  

**Steps Taken:**  
- Created and installed Meta Pixel on the Shopify store.  
- Configured key events: **Add to Cart**, **Purchase**, **View Content**.  
- Verified Pixel functionality through Meta Events Manager.  
- Used Pixel data to optimize ad targeting and measure ROI.
