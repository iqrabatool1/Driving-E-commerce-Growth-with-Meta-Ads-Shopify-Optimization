Key Learning: 
A/B testing ads improves ROI significantly.
Analytics-driven campaign decisions bring measurable growth.
E-commerce success requires both marketing and technical store optimization.


Tools Used

Meta Ads Manager: Managed and optimized Facebook & Instagram campaigns, performed A/B testing on creatives and ad copies, and tracked conversions via Meta Pixel.
Google Ads: Ran search and display campaigns to drive additional traffic and complement Meta Ads performance.
Shopify: Optimized the online store by installing smart plugins, automating workflows, improving UX, and managing the product catalog efficiently.
Meta Pixel: Implemented conversion tracking on Shopify, configured key events (Add to Cart, Purchase, View Content), and used data to optimize ad targeting and ROI.
SEO Plugins: Improved organic visibility by optimizing product pages, updating meta descriptions, and enhancing overall SEO performance.
Analytics Tools: Monitored and analyzed campaign and store performance, identifying trends, customer behavior, and areas for improvement to guide data-driven decisions.
